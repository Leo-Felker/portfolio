Leo Felker

Hello! My name is Leo Felker and I am a Freshmen at the University of Oregon. This term I am taking Computer Science 110 (Fluency With Info Tech), Women and Gender Studies 201 (Intro to Queer Studies), Comparative Literature 360 (Gender and Identity in Literature), and Philosophy 102 (Ethics). I am also a student worker at PNW Dining through University Dining Services.

In my spare time I enjoy taking walks around campus, trying new food, reading, and hanging out with my friends. You can usually find me on the bridge spanning across the Willamette River watching ducks when the sun is out. My favorite study spaces are the Erb Memorial Union balcony (when it's warm) and the Global Scholars study rooms (when I'm hungry).
